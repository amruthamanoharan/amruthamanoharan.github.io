About Me


Hello! I'm Amrutha Manoharan, a BI and Data Analytics professional with experience in Power BI, SQL, Snowflake, DAX, Power Query and Excel.

At my current role, I worked on business intelligence and reporting solutions for an international healthcare technology environment. My responsibilities included developing and improving Power BI dashboards, creating data models and DAX measures, preparing and validating data, investigating data-quality issues and translating business requirements into practical reporting solutions

I contributed to reporting across Finance, R&D, Product Lifecycle Management and Supply Chain. My work involved data from Snowflake, SQL Server, Excel and other business sources.

Before moving into business intelligence, I gained several years of experience as a Software Developer, building web applications and database-driven systems. This background gives me a strong foundation in databases, application logic, analytical problem-solving and understanding user requirements.

Through this portfolio, I share projects that demonstrate my skills in data analysis, dashboard development, data modelling, reporting and business insight generation.

Core skills: Power BI, SQL, Snowflake, DAX, Power Query, Excel, Data Modelling, Data Visualisation and Business Intelligence.
